HOW IT WORKS

From photo to full damage report in under 10 seconds.

Upload Vehicle Photo
Upload a clear image of the damaged vehicle. Multiple photos improve accuracy. No special equipment needed.

AI Damage Detection
Our YOLOv8 + EfficientNet ensemble scans the image, drawing bounding boxes around every damage region.
